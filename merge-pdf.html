What Does Merge PDF Mean?

Merging PDF is the process of combining two or more separate PDF documents into a single, unified file. This is useful when you have scanned receipts, invoices, contracts, or reports that need to be sent as one attachment. Instead of sending 10 separate files, you merge them into one PDF that keeps everything organized.

Why Use a Browser-Based PDF Merger?

Most online PDF tools upload your files to their servers for processing. This creates privacy risks — especially with sensitive documents like tax forms, medical records, or legal contracts. PDFMerge solves this by processing everything directly in your browser using the pdf-lib library. Your files never leave your device, making it the safest way to merge PDF online.
